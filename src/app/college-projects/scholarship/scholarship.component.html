SCHOLARSHIP PROGRAMMES

PTC offers three major categories of scholarships. These include:

  • New student Scholarship
    • Pentecostal Theological College offers 25% scholarsip to all new students who join our college to do new programs and is just for first semester.
  • Timothy Scholarships
    • Pentecostal Theological College 25% Timothy scholarship is offered on the whole semester college costs and is open to any qualified need student in the college.
  • PAG Church scholarship
    • Also known as Pentecostal Assemblies of God National Office 25% shepherd scholarship given on the whole semester college costs. This is strictly assistance for Pentecostal Assemblies of God church leaders.
