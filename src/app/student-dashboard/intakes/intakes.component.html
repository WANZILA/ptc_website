Academic Programmes

Pentecostal Theological College Mbale offers a number of Diploma and certificate programmes which are designed to produce Christ-like Leaders with life changing skills for church and community. The programmes are offered in three intakes of January, May and August Semesters.

Application is made through filling an application form obtained at a non refundable application fee at the Main Campus, You may also download the application form from here and pay for it on submission.
