APPLICATION PROCEDURES

Pentecostal Theological College offers a wide range of study opportunities at different levels of your career. These ranges from certificate to Diplomas. If standard study options dont apply to you, flexible study options may be more suitable. All prospective candidates to enroll in PTC will be expected to follow the laid down procedure in order to receive admission to study any of the College programmes.

  1. The application forms can be obtained at the campus, pastorate centers and at the website.
  2. The applicant will be required to fill the forms and submit to the office of the Academic Registrar Pentecostal Theological College, Mbale.
  3. The Academic admission committee will meet to select and scrutinize the application forms.
  4. The admission list will be displayed on the students Notice Board and on the website page.
  5. The students will then be given the academic calendar as they pick their admission letters from the college campus.
