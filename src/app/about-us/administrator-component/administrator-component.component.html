Dear Friends! Warm Greetings in the Name of our Lord Jesus Christ! It is with immense joy and delight that,I learn of your and keenness to learn more about the programmes offered at Pentecostal Theological College,

Mbale- Uganda. Welcome! Our Bible College has been established to equip and send forth well-trained and Spirit-filled Christian servant leaders who will work towards fulfilling the Great Commission of Jesus Christ (Matt. 28: 18-20). Our educational mandate is clearly seen in the obligation to carry out the teaching and equipping task for the church as enshrined in the Great Commission mandate. The Bible College is committed to offer scholastic training for the varied church and community leaders, with the carefully designed curriculum emphasizing; Bible based teaching that is Evangelical in Theology and Community Transformation. Hence, our College offers Diploma in Bible and Theology and Certificate in Church Ministries. PTC is a family where each person lives for the other, and all live for God. The presence of the faculty and students from various denominational, cultural, ethnic, and linguistic backgrounds enrich the community life of the college. The prayer groups, talent shows in sports, etc. helps the members to celebrate the oneness in the other. The faculty supervised fieldwork exposure provides opportunity for our students to translate the academic learning in class to practical field ministry experience. The farm in the college also provides the comprehensive approach and environment to the training that is the unique to our wholistic goal in leadership training; especially for our student pastors. Looking forward to welcoming you to the Pentecostal Theological College, Mbale. In His Mission we Go! .
